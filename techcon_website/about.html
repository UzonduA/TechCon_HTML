History

TechCon first launched in 2010 as a modest meetup of passionate developers,
and has since evolved into a major global tech innovation conference, drawing professionals,
innovators, and enthusiasts from around the world. Originally focused on software and development,
it has expanded into cross-industry themes including AI, cloud computing, cybersecurity, and web technologies.
Over the years, it has become known for its hands-on workshops,
expert-led panels, and opportunities to connect with like-minded tech creators

Mission

The mission of TechCon is to bring together the global tech community to share knowledge, spark
innovation, and inspire the future of technology. Through talks, workshops, and networking,
TechCon creates a space for learning, collaboration, and pushing boundaries in the digital world.
